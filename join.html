Want to join our team?
We are constantly seeking the brightest, most energetic, and inspired individuals to get involved in research. Technical interests include control systems, optimization, energy, vehicle electrification, batteries, and smart grids. Desirable backgrounds include civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, mathematics, and physics. Consult this student funding webpage to apply for scholarships/fellowships.

Participation can occur at the undergraduate, graduate, postdoctoral, visiting student, or visiting professor level. To apply, click the appropriate link below:

Apply for M.S./Ph.D. position
Apply for Postdoctoral Scholar position
Apply for Visiting Ph.D. or Professor position
  • Undergraduates must qualify for the CE H194 research course.
  • Masters Students must enroll in the CE 299 research course.
  • Prospective graduate students should apply directly to the UC Berkeley Graduate Division, and adhere to their deadlines and requirements.
  • All students should consult this student funding webpage to apply for scholarships/fellowships.
